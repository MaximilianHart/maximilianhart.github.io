Job Search App

This is a custom Salesforce app I built to help job seekers easily track applications, company research, and contact outreach.

Project Highlights Include:

• A custom Salesforce App for job seekers to easily track job applications & contact outreach.

• 3 flows to enable quick data entry & automated follow-up task creation.

• A Lightning Home page that gives easy access to job creation & pipeline visibility.

• Customized Search Layouts to allow working directly from a list view.
